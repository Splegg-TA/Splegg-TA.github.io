STA ELO ratings

The following table lists every player who has ever played in an official STA/EGA tournament. Matches that happened outside of STA/EGA tournaments will not be counted - neither will matches that have been played in any previous tournaments due to a lack of regulations, comparability and objectivity. A match counts once at least 1 round has officially been played. Only legitimately won, tied or lost matches count towards the ELO.

The official STA team ELO ratings - matches that include more than 2 players - can be found here. Team ELO is calculated seperately.

The ratings solely depend on who you play. The round, tournament and/or circumstances are irrelevant and do not affect the ELO standings at all. Currently the classic ELO-rating method by Arpad Elo is being used with k=40 and the range (divisor) being 300.

Updates happen after every tournament stage. If there is just one stage the ELO will be updated when over 87% of all matches have been played. Ultimately the ELO, if need be, will be updated every two months after the start of a tournament.
