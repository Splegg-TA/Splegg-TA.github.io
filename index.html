STA ELO ratings

The following table lists every player who has ever participated in an official STA tournament. Matches that happened outside of STA tournaments will not be counted - neither will matches that have been played in any previous tournaments due to a lack of regulations, comparability and objectivity. A match counts once at least 1 round has officially been played. Matches in which the opponent has violated rule No. 3 (used any kind of illegal 3rd party programs) do not count.

The official STA team ELO ratings - matches that include more than 2 players - can be found here. Team ELO is calculated seperately.

The ratings solely depend on who you play. The round, tournament and/or circumstances are irrelevant and do not affect the ELO standings at all. Currently the classic ELO-rating method by Arpad Elo is being used with k=40 and the range (divisor) being 300.

Updates happen after every tournament stage. Last update: 1-10-2019 (CET)
